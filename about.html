About Us

Founded in 1982 and headquartered in Denver, Colorado, SummitGuard Insurance has been a trusted name in protection for over four decades. We are proud to serve customers in all 50 states, providing peace of mind and financial security to families and businesses nationwide.

Our mission is to deliver innovative, reliable, and affordable insurance solutions tailored to the unique needs of every customer. With a commitment to integrity and service, we strive to make insurance simple, accessible, and transparent.

From our humble beginnings as a local agency, we've grown into a national provider, but our values remain rooted in community, trust, and excellence. Join the thousands who rely on SummitGuard to protect what matters most.
